Biography

Shin Ryujin, born on April 17, 2001, in Seoul, South Korea, is a dynamic performer and a key member of the renowned K-Pop girl group ITZY under JYP Entertainment. Known for her fierce dance moves, commanding rap verses, and captivating stage presence, Ryujin has carved a name for herself as a powerhouse in the fourth generation of K-Pop. Her journey from appearing on reality shows like MIXNINE to leading ITZY's success is a testament to her talent and determination.

Major Accomplishments

  • Debut with ITZY (2019): Contributed to iconic hits like Dalla Dalla, Wannabe, and Loco.
  • Dance Icon: Her viral shoulder dance in Wannabe became a worldwide trend.
  • Awards & Recognition: Multiple wins with ITZY, including Rookie of the Year awards and top charting songs.
  • Role Model: Celebrated for her confidence, individuality, and authenticity on and off stage.
  • Versatility: A skilled dancer, charismatic rapper, and a creative soul admired by fans globally.
